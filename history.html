History will be kind to us as we intend to create it



In 2003 a team of scientists wanted to explore the limits of the possible. They decided to revolutionize Pharmaceutical Research, transform and bring positive change in lives of the people. Thus was born Optimus. Starting with technical consulting assignments for other pharmaceutical companies in India and abroad we came to be recognized for our expertise in manufacturing new molecules. Our reputation is built on a decade of our research achievements, strictest compliance to quality standards and timely deliverables to our clients. The passion and energy with which we started Optimus has guided our journey from a technical consultation firm to the elevated status of a custom manufacturing associate. Today they glow even brighter as we enter a new phase of growth and opportunities with confidence and optimism.



Hghlights of Journey so far

  • Started by a team of technocrats with R & D centre in 2004
  • Expanded facility by establishing multipurpose commercial plant as per cGMP norms in 2006
  • Filed several patents on various products from different therapeutic segments in 2008
  • Facility was inspected and certified by WHO GMP and ISO 9001:2008 between 2008-2010
  • Approved by several MNCs for their advanced Intermediates for generic markets between 2011-2012
  • Filed KDMF, EDMF and CEP to regulatory authority 2013
