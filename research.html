R & D

  • Home
  • R & D






Pharmaceutical research is a field where success of an organization is often determined by the depth its R&D. We, at Optimus relentlessly distinguish ourselves from our larger competitors through innovation. Fortunately, the most important ingredient in this pursuit is “brilliant minds” which Optimus has proudly grown and nurtured over the past 10 years. There are very few things that give immense satisfaction as an innovative research does. It is only natural that our researchers love what they do and accomplish. We have a proud tradition of nurturing research since its very inception. We have furthered this recently through collaborations with academic and chemical research institutions. We are very well aware that in the arena of competitive Pharmaceutical industry requiring deep R&D Infrastructure, having clever researchers is not enough; to have the right strategy and staying a step ahead of others is crucial. We are proud of our research accomplishments and we look forward to many more R&D challenges in the future, with a great level of confidence and enthusiasm.


Research and Development Capabilities

  • Chiral Synthesis
