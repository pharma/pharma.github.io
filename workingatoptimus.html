Together let's make this world a better place


If you have come here after knowing what we do, you probably know already what we expect from those who wish to be part of Optimus. A brief note for the rest. We intend to revolutionize pharmaceutical research; naturally we are interested in fellow revolutionaries. We need you to actively seek responsibility and ownership (and demonstrate your potential in doing so during your interactions with us). We value trust and teamwork above everything; we expect you to do so as well. Ours is a fast growing organization and innovation is its engine of growth. Great, if you are a proven innovator; if not already, at least you must possess insatiable curiosity which, with the right kind of mentoring, could be converted into innovativeness. Last but not the least, you should know how to smile (prove it!), for that's the best way to face challenges.



What you get

  • Chance to touch the lives of a million people.
  • Grow with Optimus, grow in reputation.
  • Work with one of the best in the field
  • Great team mates and work culture.
  • People who are willing to learn from you too.
  • Salary, perks and bonus? Rest assured, we pay on par as per industry standards, if not more! (anything else?)
