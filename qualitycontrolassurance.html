Quality Compliance/ Quality Management System

Optimus Quality Management System includes Quality Assurance and Regulatory Affairs, where the functionality and the accountability of each unit is separated from the other. Optimus established Quality Compliance as per Schedule M, ICH Q7 and 21 CFR 210-211 for the operation in cGMP production of API's. Following are some of our International accreditations and clients audits.

  • Certified by ISO 9001:2008
  • Certified by WHO GMP
  • Successful client audits by Global Pharmaceutical Companies like Novartis, Pfizer etc.
  • Under process of filing COS/ CEP's, EDMF and USDMF's.

Quality Assurance

Optimus Quality Assurance is responsible for the site specific maintenance, compliance, realization and further development of internal Quality Management System. Quality Assurance ensures that API's and Advanced Intermediates produced from various stages of manufacturing to comply with cGMP requirements. All the relevant departments are complying with cGMP standards. Following are few important activities handled by Quality Assurance team.

  • Document control & Annual Product reviews
  • Monitoring of all validations including cleaning validations
  • Successful client audits by Global Pharmaceutical Companies like Novartis, Pfizer etc.
  • Under process of filing COS/ CEP's, EDMF and USDMF's.
