Built over a sprawling 13 acre plot, the manufacturing facility has currently 4 production blocks with adequate extra space for future expansion. The facility employs more than 350 personnel comprising of more than 100 scientific personnel only in R & D, Analytical and Quality.

Following are the reactions currently being carried out. Of course, we are constantly increasing our capabilities.

  • Grignard and Organometallic Reaction
  • Chiral Synthesis
  • Nucleophilic Substitution
  • Oxidation
  • Friedel Crafts Reactions
  • Halogenation (Bromination and Chlorination)
  • Condensation
  • Cyanation
  • Selective Reduction
  • Hydrogenation and Asymmetric Reduction
  • Organometallic Reactions
