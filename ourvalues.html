  • Values and Culture


Success of our partners is something we strive for. Our efforts are guided by good corporate values and practices. Ethical conduct, responsibility to society, robust integrity, nurturing talent and sustainable growth define our core values. We aim at nothing less than being a model for running an enterprise without compromising on values. We are proud of our values, for they keep us grounded!

Quality

We are relentless in our pursuit for quality. For us quality and achievements are not mere numbers and certificates; we are driven by a higher goal: try and make the best and most effective drugs available to the medical fraternity and the public. Towards this we have as our mission to have the best equipment, the best talents and the best internal standards

Commitment

Our purpose is to grow with a purpose. We are committed to pursuing cutting-edge pharmaceutical research towards saving and improving lives. We are determined to show that talent combined with dedication makes things possible. We intend to measure success based on numbers - the number of people whom we impact positively. We have deep concern and sensitivity for our ecology; hence we are committed to pursuing growth within the framework of eco-friendliness.

Innovation

This is one word, but it means many things to us. This is what keeps us one step ahead. This reminds us of what is ahead of us. This is what we are good at. This is what ensures fairness. This helps us find talent and talent to find us. This is what realizes our dreams. This sets our bar high. This is what keeps us focused. This is fun for us. This is important for the success of our partners. This is what brings us respect.

Trust & Team work

“Whole-more-than-sum-of-parts” is what we strongly believe in. We believe that an environment of trust is a must for progress and success. Great teams are those in which individual goals are aligned with a common purpose. Trust allows sharing of responsibilities. Team provides trustworthy talent to share responsibilities with. We don't ask which comes first: trust or team. We leave paradoxes to philosophers. For us trust and team go together. Earning the trust of our partners and sustaining it is our ceaseless endeavor.

Outreach

Though we have worked very hard for our success, we were helped along the way by a great many known and unknown factors. Considerate business partners and financial houses, understanding families and large hearted friends, government policies and social environment nudged us and encouraged us knowingly and unknowingly in every step that we took. We are indebted to these great individuals, our institutions and our society at large, for making us what we are today. It is only fitting that we give back in a small measure what we ourselves have received. As a start, we identified bright youngsters and provided them with opportunities that we wish we had gotten when we started. We have miles to go in this journey and more lives to touch upon.
